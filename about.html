Hi. I’m Matt.

I’m a design-minded web geek living in Champaign, Illinois. Yes, we invented the web browser. Yes, you can be jealous.

I work as a Front-End Web Developer with my amazing team at Apartment Therapy and The Kitchn. Like a growing number of web teams, we operate remotely and I work from home. Most days it’s pretty great, but the upsides of remote work are offset by unique challenges too.

On this site I write about my experiences in web design and development. It’s probably going to get pretty nerdy (sorry Mom). I’m also dipping my toes into speaking publicly on the topic. Hit me up on Twitter if you’re looking for a presenter.

At home I brew my own beer and usually it’s pretty drinkable, which is a bonus. I also like to find ridiculous icons, and occasionally post them to Tumblr.
